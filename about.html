About CryptoDash

CryptoDash is a real-time cryptocurrency market tracking dashboard designed to provide users with live crypto prices, market trends, price history and portfolio insights in a simple and interactive way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make cryptocurrency data easy to understand by presenting real-time market information through a clean and user-friendly dashboard.

Features

  • Live cryptocurrency price tracking
  • Top gainers and top losers analysis
  • 7 days price history visualization
  • USD to INR currency conversion
  • Favorite coins management using local storage
  • Dark mode experience

Technology Used

CryptoDash is built using modern frontend technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, REST APIs and Chart.js for interactive market visualization.

How It Works

The dashboard fetches real-time cryptocurrency data from public APIs and displays important market information such as current prices, percentage changes, rankings and historical trends.
